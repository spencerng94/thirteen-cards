# Privacy Policy 2026 Update Summary

**Date:** January 06, 2026

This document summarizes the updates made to the Privacy Policy to comply with 2026 App Store and Google Play requirements.

## Updates Made

### 1. ✅ Google API Services Disclosure (NEW SECTION)

**Location**: Added as a new section after "Data Security" and before "Third-Party Services"

**Content Added**:
- Mandatory "Limited Use" disclosure for Google OAuth
- Explicit statement that use of Google API data adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy
- Clear list of how Google API data is used (authentication, profile management, customer support)
- Statement that Google API data is not sold or shared except as necessary for services

**Compliance**: Satisfies Google's requirement for apps using Google OAuth to include the Limited Use disclosure.

### 2. ✅ Personalized Advertising (AdMob) - ENHANCED

**Location**: Enhanced existing "Google AdMob" section under "Third-Party Services"

**Content Added**:
- Explicit mention of "Google Mobile Ads SDK"
- Detailed list of data collected: Device Identifiers (IDFA/AAID), IP addresses, device information, ad interaction data
- Clear explanation of how data is used (personalized ads, fraud prevention, analytics)
- Link to Google's Advertising Privacy & Terms
- Instructions for opting out of personalized advertising

**Compliance**: Satisfies App Store and Google Play requirements for apps using personalized advertising.

### 3. ✅ Account Deletion & Data Retention - ENHANCED

**Location**: Enhanced existing "Deletion" section under "Your Rights and Choices"

**Content Added**:
- **Step-by-step instructions** for in-app account deletion (Settings → Account → Delete Account)
- **Explicit list** of what gets deleted:
  - Profile information
  - Game statistics
  - In-game currency (Gems and Coins)
  - Virtual items
  - Social data
  - Achievement data
  - Gameplay preferences
- **Data retention exceptions** (legal requirements, legitimate business purposes, anonymized data)
- **Processing time** information (immediate processing, 30-day backup retention)
- **Irreversible action** warning

**Compliance**: Satisfies App Store and Google Play requirements for clear account deletion policies.

### 4. ✅ Children's Privacy (COPPA/GDPR-K) - ENHANCED

**Location**: Enhanced existing "Children's Privacy" section

**Content Added**:
- **Explicit age restriction** (13 and older)
- **Detailed procedures** for handling accidental data collection from children under 13
- **Parental rights** section with contact information
- **Clear statements** about what we do NOT do (collect from under-13s, allow under-13 accounts, market to children)
- **Guidance for minors** (13-18) to review with parents

**Compliance**: Satisfies COPPA (US) and GDPR-K (EU) requirements for children's privacy protection.
